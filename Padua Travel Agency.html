Palawan Phillippines

Palawan is located in the Phillippines which is located in the region of MIMAROPA. It has also been names the largest province in the country. Palawan is famous for it's white sandy beaches and clear blue water, it is also known for its diversity regarding culture. These cultures include, Asian, African and Spanish.Palawan has a large variety of wildlife inhabitting its island such as, water buffalo, horses, cats, monkeys, lemurs and mongoose and so many more. This is a great place to relax with family or by yourself they also have many tour packages you can use during your vacation to further explore and learn about the island.
